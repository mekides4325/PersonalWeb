A nation of festivals


Ethiopian is a country full of vibrant and colorful festivals. The biggest, Timket, is a three-day annual festival that honours the baptism of jesus christ in the river jordan. Today the priest remove the 'Tabots' from each church and march to the nearest water source, where the communal baptism takes place. The procession is accompainied by thousands of local dressed in dazzling white traditional dress that contrasts with the colours of the ceremonial robes and sequined velvet umbrellas of the priest.
